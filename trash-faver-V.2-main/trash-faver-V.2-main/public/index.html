Trash Fever

By Mattew

The best videogame to learn recycling

How to play

  • For game Trash Fever need to click the trash to choose where to throw the waste for do trash recycling.
  • If you put the waste in the correct trash you will get points.
  • If you have in the series of nine waste thrown correctly, you can click to progress bar to unlock the HOLE.
  • The HOLE is a black hole where you can throw all the waste, to help you in the game.
